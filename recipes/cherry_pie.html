Cherry Pie

Cherry pie is a pie baked with a cherry filling. Traditionally, cherry pie is made with sour cherries rather than sweet cherries.

  • 4 cups pitted fresh sour cherries
  • 1-1/2 cups sugar
  • 3 tsp MINUTE Tapioca
  • 1.4 tsp. almond extract
  • 1 pkg. ready to use pie crusts
  • 1 Tbsp. butter or margarine, cut up
